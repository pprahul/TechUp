An exciting contest for undergrad students in computer science, to build, showcase and grab prizes.

Who Can Apply?

Open to 1st and 2nd year undergrad students currently studying in Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.
A passionate   mind and code-working   hands is an added advantage.

Prizes.     Lots of them.

You are going to be rewarded.


20,000 In Cash* sponsored handsomely by CS alumni.
Exclusive Mentoring Hours (Priceless) with some of the best in industry and academia.
Intern Opportunities** at some of the best places you would want to work in.

* Prize money for each team will be based on their performance.
** Best performers will be referred to companies for doing internships.

How to start?

 Sign Up.
Fill in the Google form and tell us you are in for the challenge.
 Get Mentor.
You will be assigned a mentor based on your interest area.
 Code.
Start coding your app either for Mobile or Web. Details below.
 Submit.
Upload your app in our portal before 1 July.

Details

Rules

  Team Size
A maximum of upto 3 students can be part of a team.
  Technologies
Android, Web Frameworks (Django, Ruby On Rails etc.), node.js and bootstrap / boilerplate.

  Create
a private git repo at BitBucket and share it with your mentor. (If you are new to BitBucket, it's alright. Start by creating an account and read this.)
  Web Page
Design a simplistic web page which will showcase your app, preferably at github.io
  Blog
Keep everyone updated about your app's progress. Create a blog at wordpress, blogger, or your favourite place.
  Help 'O' Help
From start to finish our mentors will provide you guidance and help. So, don't be scared to start off.
