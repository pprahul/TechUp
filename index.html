TechUp. This Summer.

An exciting contest for undergrad students in computer science, to build, showcase and grab prizes.

Who can apply?

Who Can Apply?

Any undergrad student currently studying in Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. Yes, that's the only requirement.
A passionate   mind and code-working   hands is an added advantage.

Prizes.     Lots of them.

You are going to be rewarded.


In Cash sponsored handsomely by CS alumni.
Exclusive Mentoring Hours (Priceless) with some of the best in industry and academia.
Intern Opportunities at some of the best places you would want to work in.
Jump In

How to start?

 Sign Up.
Fill in the Google form and tell us you are in for the challenge.
 Code.
Start coding your app either for Android or Web using Java / HTML5 / CSS3.
 Submit.
Upload your app in our portal before 1 July.

Rules

  Team Size
A maximum of upto 3 students can be part of a team.
  Technologies
Java, Android, HTML5, CSS3, Python, PHP or any other mobile / web app technologies can be used.

  Upload
The final code / product needs to uploaded at Github. (If you are new to github, it's alright. Start by creating an account there.)
  Web Page
Your app should have a web page which advertises the app, and it's functionalities, preferably at github.io
